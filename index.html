Spotlight: Super Cool Game

Released in 1992, Super Cool Game took the world by storm with its innovative gameplay and stunning 16-bit graphics. It remains a fan favorite among retro gaming enthusiasts.

Cheat Codes and Tips

Struggling to beat the final boss? Here are some tips to help you out:

  • Secret Level: Press Up, Up, Down, Down, Left, Right, Left, Right, B, A at the title screen.
  • Extra Lives: Collect 100 coins to earn an extra life.
